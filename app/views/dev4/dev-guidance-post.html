How to post your paper fit note

Posting your fit note means it could take longer for us to process your claim.

You do not need to post your fit note if you've uploaded it online.

If you cannot send it to us online you can post it, without a stamp, to:

  • Freepost
  • DWP ESA 26

Talk to an advisor

  • Telephone: 0800 169 0310
  • Textphone: 0800 169 0314
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 5pm (except public holidays)
