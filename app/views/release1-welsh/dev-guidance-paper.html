How to take a photo of your fit note

  1. You can use a smartphone, camera or tablet. Or you can use a scanner connected to a computer.
  2. You must hold your smartphone, tablet or camera in landscape mode (sideways) when taking the photo. The fit note must also be landscape
  3. The whole fit note must be shown. It needs to be on 1 page.
  4. Check that the image is in focus and all the text can be read.
