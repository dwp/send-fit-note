There is a problem with
the image of your fit note




Your fit note cannot be accepted because:

The image is too light or too dark to read

If you have a paper fit note, take another photo, making sure the fit note is well lit.

Some corners of the image cannot be seen

The top right and bottom right corners of your fit note image cannot be seen.

This could be because the image is too dark or the photo was taken at an angle.

If you have a paper fit note, take another photo, making sure the whole fit note is well lit and visible.

Your fit note might be accepted but could take longer to process because:

The data matrix cannot be read

The data matrix is the small square of black and white dots at the bottom of your fit note. If it is missing or if it cannot be read by our system, your fit note will have to be processed manually.

If you have a paper fit note, take another photo, making sure the data matrix is clear and in focus.

The unique ID cannot be read

Your fit note should have the unique ID ‘Med 3’. If it’s missing or if it cannot be read by our system, your fit note could be rejected.

Check the unique ID on the bottom left of your fit note.

If you have a paper fit note, take another photo making sure the whole fit note is visible.


If you cannot upload your fit note, you can post it (opens in new tab).
