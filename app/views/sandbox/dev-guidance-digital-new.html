Before you upload your digital fit note

1. Check your fit note is valid

Your fit note should have the unique ID 'Med 3' at the bottom left - if this is missing, or if our system cannot read it, your fit note could be rejected.




The small black and white square (the data matrix) at the bottom left of your fit note should be clear - if our system cannot read it, your fit note could take longer to process.

2. Check your fit note will be accepted

Everything is on 1 page (the fit note is not double-sided).




All the words, incuding your contact details, are clear and easy to read.

The fit note on the left would be rejected because it is not readable.




The whole of the fit note is visible, including all four corners.

The fit note on the left would be rejected because the corners are missing.




The image is either portrait or landscape




What to do if your fit note has a password

If you need to enter a password to see your fit note, our system will not be able to read it and it will be rejected.

If possible, you should save your fit note as a new file without a password and upload this new version.

You will need to enter your NI number after you have uploaded your fit note. You can find this on your benefit letter, payslip or P60.
