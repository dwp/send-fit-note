Before you upload your digital fit note

It is important that your fit note can be read by our system, or it could be rejected.

Before you upload your fit note, make sure:

  1. it is all on 1 page
  2. it is not protected by a password
  3. the whole fit note is visible, including all four corners
  4. everything can be read easily, including your contact details
  5. if it has a data matrix (a black and white square that looks like a QR code), this is clear and not blurred

What to do if your fit note has a password

If you need to enter a password to see your fit note, our system will not be able to read it and it will be rejected.

If possible, you should save your fit note as a new file without a password and upload this new version.
