If you have photographed or scanned a paper fit note

Your fit note cannot be uploaded because:

The image is too light or too dark to read

Take another photo, making sure the fit note is well lit.

The image is not clear and readable

Take another photo, making sure the fit note is in focus.

The data matrix cannot be read

The data matrix is a small square of black and white dots at the bottom of the fit note. If it cannot be read, your fit note could take longer to process. Take another photo, making sure the data matrix is clear and in focus.
