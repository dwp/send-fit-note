What to do if you cannot upload your fit note

If your fit note is not accepted, you'll see an error message telling you what to do.

If the problem continues, you can print a paper copy of the fit note and post it (you do not need a stamp) to:

  • Freepost
  • DWP ESA 26

You do not need to post your fit note if you've uploaded it successfully.

You should send your National Insurance number with your fit note. You can find this on your benefit letter, payslip or P60.

Talk to an advisor

  • Telephone: 0800 169 0310
  • Textphone: 0800 169 0314
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 5pm (except public holidays)
