Before you upload your digital fit note

It is important that your fit note can be read by our system, or it could be rejected.

If it contains a data matrix (a black and white square that looks like a QR code), it should be clear.

Before you upload it, make sure:

  1. all the text can be read easily
  2. it is on 1 page with your contact details visible
  3. the whole fit note is visible, including all four corners


Check that your fitnote is correct

Your fit note should have the unique ID ‘Med 3’. If it’s missing or if it cannot be read by our system, your fit note could be rejected.

Check the unique ID on the bottom left of your fit note.
