Before you upload your digital fit note

Check that your fit note is valid

  1. Your fit note should have the unique ID ‘Med 3’ at the bottom left. If this is missing or if our system cannot read it, your fit note could be rejected.



  2. The small black and white square (called a data matrix) at the bottom left of your fit note should be clear. If our system cannot read it, your fit note could take longer to process.





Check that your fit note will be accepted

Your fit note will be rejected if:

  1. it is double-sided
  2. it is protected by a password – you should save your fit note as a new file before you upload it

Your fit note should be accepted if:

  1. all the information is on 1 page
  2. all the words, including your contact details, are clear and easy to read
  3. the image is either landscape or portrait
  4. the whole fit note is visible, including all four corners





You will need to enter your National Insurance number after you have uploaded your fit note. You can find this on your benefit letter, payslip or P60.
