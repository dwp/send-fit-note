You’ll be told during your application for Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) or ‘new style’ ESA, when you need to send fit notes (sometimes called ‘sick notes’ or a ‘statement of fitness for work’).

Check which healthcare professionals can give you a fit note. You can either send your fit note online or post it to us.

How to send your fit note online

You'll need your National Insurance number and either:

  • a digital fit note that's been sent to you by email or that you've downloaded from a web link, completed by the healthcare professional
  • a printed (paper) fit note, signed by the healthcare professional - you'll need to take a photo of it or scan it so that you can send it online
This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
Start now

How to post your fit note to us

You do not need a stamp.
