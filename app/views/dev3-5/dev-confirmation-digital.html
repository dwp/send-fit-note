What happens next

Keep your fit note safe, do not throw it away.

It can take up to 24 hours for you to receive a text confirming we've got your fit note (Monday to Friday, 8am to 8pm). You do not need to check its progress. If there's a problem, we'll contact you.

You will need to keep sending us fit notes as long as you are unfit for work. For example, if your fit note is for 14 days, you will need to send us another one when it runs out if you're still unfit for work. We will tell you when you can stop sending them.
